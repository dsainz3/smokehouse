# Whiskey River Marinade

## Canonical Base (Do Not Modify)

**Ratio-based:** use these parts.

- 1 part cheap whiskey (Canadian preferred)
- 2 parts soy sauce
- 2 tbsp garlic (minced/crushed)

## Timing Guidelines

- Beef: 30–90 minutes (2 hours max)
- Pork chops: 45 minutes – 2 hours (up to 4 hours for thick chops)
- Chicken: 30 minutes – 2 hours (1 hour ideal)

## Approved Variants

### Pork Chops

- Add **1/2 part water** to reduce intensity

### Chicken

- Add **1 part water**
- Optional: **1 tbsp oil** (helps surface browning)
