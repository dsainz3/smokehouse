# Smoked Sweet Potatoes

## Context

- Holiday: Thanksgiving
- Smoker: Pit Boss Austin XL
- Cook Style: Smoke
- Wood: Apple or mild blend

## Prep

- Wash and dry sweet potatoes
- Pierce each potato 3–4 times with a fork
- Optional: light oil rub

## Cook

### Smoke (Whole)

- Temp: **250°F**
- Time: **2½–3 hours**
- Target internal temp: **205°F**
- Turn once halfway through

### Smoke (Foil-Wrapped – Faster)

- Temp: **250°F**
- Time: **1½–2 hours**
- Wrap tightly in foil
- Target internal temp: **205°F**

## Notes

- Unwrapped = more smoke, firmer skin
- Foil-wrapped = softer texture, faster cook
- Rest 10 minutes before serving

## Feedback & Ratings

- ⭐ Rating (1–5): 4
- Would make again? Yes
- What worked well: Hands-off timing during holiday cooks
- Improvements: Consider size-matching potatoes for even finish
- Variations / recommendations: Butter + salt (savory) or brown sugar + cinnamon (sweet)
