# Smoked Green Bean Casserole

## Context

- Holiday: Thanksgiving
- Smoker: Pit Boss Austin XL
- Cook Style: Smoke
- Wood: Apple or mild blend

## Ingredients

- 2 lbs fresh green beans, trimmed
- 1½ cups cream of mushroom soup
- ½ cup heavy cream
- 1 tsp garlic powder
- ½ tsp black pepper
- Salt to taste
- 1½ cups breadcrumbs
- 3 tbsp melted butter
- 1½ cups crispy fried onions

## Prep

1. Blanch green beans 3–4 minutes. Drain well.
2. Mix soup, cream, and seasoning.
3. Fold in green beans.
4. Transfer to smoker-safe dish.
5. Mix breadcrumbs with melted butter.
6. Top casserole with breadcrumbs and **half** the fried onions.

## Cook

- Temp: **250°F**
- Time: **60–75 minutes**
- Add remaining fried onions during last 15 minutes

## Notes

- No cheese — keeps it classic and clean.
- Breadcrumb topping gives better texture than onions alone.
- Tent loosely with foil if edges dry.
