# Spatchcock Smoked Cornish Game Hens

## Context

- Holiday: Christmas
- Smoker: Pit Boss Austin XL
- Cook Style: Smoke + Hot Finish
- Wood: Apple or cherry

## Prep

- Spatchcock hens (see ../../techniques/spatchcocking.md)
- Pat dry
- Butter rub under and over skin (see ../../techniques/butter_under_skin.md)
- Season with salt, pepper, garlic powder, smoked paprika, poultry seasoning

## Cook

- Temp: **275°F**
- Time: **60–75 minutes**
- Target temps:
  - Breast: **160°F**
  - Thigh: **170–175°F**
- Finish: raise to **400°F** for **8–12 minutes** (see ../../techniques/hot_finish.md)

## Rest

- Rest **5–10 minutes** (avoid tight foil if you want crisp skin)

## Feedback & Ratings

- ⭐ Rating (1–5): 5
- Would make again? Yes
- What worked well: Even cook from spatchcock; crisp skin after hot finish
- Improvements: None noted
- Variations / recommendations: Keep hens similar size; legs toward hotter right side
